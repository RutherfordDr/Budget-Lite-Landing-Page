Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Will Apple be supported?

Due to the cost of an Apple developer license, Budget-Lite will not be on the Apple store. Potentially in the future it will be supported.

What devices are supported for Android?

Over 19,000 android devices are supported. The app is optimized to work on both phone and tablet alike. If your device is not supported, feel free to contact SecureBudgetDevContact@gmail.com.

What countries is Budget-Lite supported in?

Budget-Lite is supported in Canada, the U.S as well as the U.K. Further country support is in development.

What do I do if I have found a bug?

Please send a bug report to SecureBudgetDevContact@gmail.com. Add reproduction steps and a screenshot with a brief summary of the bug if possible.
